Stopping Potential جهد الأيقاف

The electric potential (voltage) at which the photoelectrons stop flowing; when photocurrent becomes zero, it is called stopping potential.

Examples
1. The stopping potential multiplied by the electron charge measures the kinetic energy of one electron.
2. The greater the frequency of light, the greater is the stopping potential.
3. For values of frequency less than threshold frequency ( v0 ) stopping potential is negative.

إنه الجهد الكهربائي (الجهد) الذي تتوقف عنده الإلكترونات الضوئية عن التدفق ؛ ومن ثم يصبح التيار الضوئي صفراً .
