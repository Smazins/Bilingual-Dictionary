Wave packet: حزمة الموجة

A mathematical hypothesis used in quantum mechanics to describe the localized behaviour of a wave-like particle. It represents a particle's position and momentum.

Notes: Photon is an example of wave-like particle.

Examples
1. In states where a quantum mechanical particle is bound, it must be localized as a wave packet.
2. If a particle is a wave packet, we will not be able to measure its exact position.
3. The speed of the wave packets is called the group velocity.

فرضية رياضية تستخدم في ميكانيكا الكم لوصف السلوك الموضعي لجسيم يشبه الموجة. إنه يمثل موضع الجسيم وزخمه.

ملاحظات: الفوتون هو مثال للجسيم الموجي.
