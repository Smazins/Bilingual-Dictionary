Energy Quantization: تكميم الطاقة

the principle that energy is quantized meaning it has only discrete values in certain physical systems, particularly at the atomic and subatomic levels. It is a key concept in quantum physics.

Notes: In an atom, the energy of each orbit is quantized; hence, energy can be absorbed or emitted by electrons only in integral multiples of a small unit of energy, known as a quantum.

Examples
1. The wave nature of matter causes the quantization of orbital energy in atoms.
2. Planck's work on quantization of energy was awarded the Nobel Prize in 1918.
3. The quantization of light energy into discrete packets suggests that each packet is a light particle or photon.
