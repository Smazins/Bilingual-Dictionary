Black Body Radiation: إشعاع الجسم الأسود

A hypothetical black body which completely absorbs the electromagnetic radiation falling on it and emits the radiation after reaching the equilibrium temperature.

Notes: Black Body Radiation is a key concept in physics, introduced by Max Planck in 1901. Plank’s work on the black body radiation is a pivotal discovery in Quantum Physics. It has enhanced our understanding of the quantized energy levels of an atom.

Examples
1. If there is an infinite number of frequencies, the radiation is known as black body radiation.
2. Kirchhoff's work on black body radiation was fundamental in the development of quantum theory.
3. All radiation absorbed by the atmosphere is converted into black body radiation quite rapidly.
