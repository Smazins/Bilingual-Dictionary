Neutron :النيوترون

A subatomic particle without an electric charge which is found in the nucleus of an atom. It is slightly bigger than the proton. Together with protons, neutrons make up the nucleus of an atom.

Notes: Neutrons were discovered by James Chadwick in 1932.

Examples
1. A neutron decays to produce a proton, an electron, and an electron anti-neutrino.
2. Neutrons are uncharged and possess the property of spin.
3. Neutrons are subatomic particles found in the nuclei of all normal atoms except hydrogen.
