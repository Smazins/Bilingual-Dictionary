Electron Spin: دوران الإلكترون

A fundamental property of electrons. Electrons can have one of two possible spin states: "spin up" or "spin down."

Notes: Electrons spin about their own axis as our planet Earth spins.

Examples
1. Magnets are caused by millions of electrons spinning in the same direction.
2. Two electrons spinning in the same direction, having the same spin axis, have the strongest magnetic attraction, to each other.
3. If two electrons are spinning in the same direction, the magnetic field is stronger.
