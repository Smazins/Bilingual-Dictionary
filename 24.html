Quantum Numbers: عدد الكمية

The parameters used to describe the location of atomic and subatomic particles in an atom.

Notes: There are four quantum numbers, namely: principal quantum number (n) which indicates the orbit of an atom; azimuthal quantum number (l) which describes the shape of the orbital; magnetic quantum number (ml) which specifies the orientation of orbital, and spin quantum number (ms) which describes the spin of the electron.

Examples
1. Quantum numbers occur only in certain combinations of values.
2. Quantum numbers are used to describe the orbitals in which electrons can be found.
3. No two electrons can have the same four quantum numbers.
