Coherent Scattering of photon - Thomson Scattering تناثر متماسك - تناثر الفوتونات - تناثر طومسون

During scattering, Coherent or Thomson scattering occurs if the photon has the same energy ) Ei=Es)before and after the collision with the particle encountered and it is deflected from its original path.

Examples
1. Thomson scattering involves photons and free electrons.
2. Thomson scattering is the elastic scattering of electromagnetic radiation by a free charged particle.
3. Thomson scattering is the classical theory which postulates that an electromagnetic wave is scattered by charged particles.

أثناء تناثر الفوتونات (التشتت) ، يحدث تناثر متماسك (تناثر طومسون) إذا كان للفوتون نفس الطاقة ( Ei=Es) قبل وبعد الاصطدام بالجسيم الذي تمت مواجهته وانحرافه عن مساره الأصلي.
