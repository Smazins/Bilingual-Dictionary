In physics, quantum theory, also known as quantum mechanics or quantum physics, deals with the study of interaction between matter and electromagnetic radiation at the atomic and subatomic levels.

Notes: Quantum Theory is one of the pillars of modern science developed over the last hundred years. Semiconductors, lasers, nuclear reactors, and quantum computers have been developed based on Quantum Theory.

Examples
1. The Compton Effect can be explained on the basis of the quantum theory.
2. A basic part of quantum theory is wave-particle duality.
3. The idea of quantum field theory began in the late 1920s with British physicist Paul Dirac.
