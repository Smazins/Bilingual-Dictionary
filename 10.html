Incoherent Scattering of photon - Compton Scattering تناثر غير متماسك - تناثر الفوتونات - تناثر كومبتون

During this type of scattering, the scattered photons have two mechanisms. In the first mechanism, both the incident and scattered photon have the same energy (Ei=Es). In the second component, the energy of the incident photon is slightly higher than that of the incident photon (Ei>Es) in addition to the deflection from the original path after the collision

Examples
1. In the Compton scattering process, the photon loses some of its energy.
2. Compton scattering is a photon activity that results from a photon and electron attraction.
3. Compton scattering usually refers to the interaction involving only the electrons of an atom.
4. Compton scattering is commonly described as inelastic scattering, because the energy in the scattered photon is less than the energy of the incident photon. (google)
