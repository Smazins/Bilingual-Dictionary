Photo Current التيار الضوئي

The current produced by the Photoelectrons during the photoelectric effect is called photo current.

Examples
1. Photocurrent is the most important parameter in a photovoltaic cell.
2. Photocurrent depends both on intensity and frequency of incident beam.
3. Photocurrent decreases with the increase in the frequency of the incident photon.

التيار الذي تنتجه الإلكترونات الضوئية أثناء التأثير الكهروضوئي يسمى التيار الضوئي.
