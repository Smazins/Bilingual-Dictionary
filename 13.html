Frequency التردد

Frequency (f) of a wave is defined as the number of full waves/oscillations completed in one second of time. It is measured in Hertz (Hz) or s-1.

Examples
1. The frequency of a wave is generally expressed in cm or m.
2. The frequencies of radio waves are low.
3. The frequency of matter waves, as deduced by de Broglie, is directly proportional to the total energy of a particle.

فيتم تعريف التردد (f) للموجة على أنه عدد الموجات / الذبذبات الكاملة التي تكتمل في ثانية واحدة من الزمن. يقاس بالهرتز (هرتز) أو s-1.
