Proton:بروتون

A subatomic particle that carries a positive electric charge and found in the nucleus of an atom. It is almost 1,836 times bigger than an electron.

Notes: Protons are essential for defining the atomic number of an element. Protons were discovered by E. Rutherford in 1909.

Examples
1. The atoms of all elements are made up of protons, neutrons, and electrons.
2. Each atom has a charged substructure consisting of a nucleus, which is made of protons and neutrons surrounded by electrons.
3. Protons repel each other because they are positively charged, and charges of the same kind repel.

جسيم دون ذري يحمل شحنة كهربائية موجبة ويوجد في نواة الذرة. وهو أكبر بحوالي 1836 مرة من الإلكترون.

ملاحظات: البروتونات ضرورية لتحديد العدد الذري للعنصر. تم اكتشاف البروتونات بواسطة إ. رذرفورد في عام 1909.
