Saturation Current تيار التشبع

During the photoelectric effect, when all the electrons are flowing from cathode to anode the photocurrent reaches its maximum limit which is called Saturation Current.

Examples
1. A p-n junction has a saturation current of 3.60 mA.
2. The saturation current is reached when all the ejected electrons are able to reach the anode.
3. The magnitude of saturation photoelectric current depends upon intensity.

أثناء التأثير الكهروضوئي ، عندما تتدفق جميع الإلكترونات من الكاثود إلى القطب الموجب ، يصل التيار الضوئي إلى أقصى حد له وهو ما يسمى تيار التشبع.
