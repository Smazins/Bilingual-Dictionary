Photon: جسيم موجة

According to the quantum theory of radiation, a photon is a small wave pocket of electromagnetic radiation. It behaves like a massless particle and moves at a speed of 3×108 m/s in vacuum.

Notes: Photon is also known as quantum.

Examples
1. The energy of a photon is related to its frequency and wavelength.
2. Photons have energy and momentum and they also have wave characteristics, such as wavelength and frequency.
3. A photon is a particle of light.
